IOT Based Smart Traafic System


This system will be proposed for reducing traffic jams on signals. It will save the time of people and reduces accidents in traffics. It will overcome the problems which are generated in existing system.

It will also reduce the manual management of traffic lights on roads. This system will definately give a proper way of management for traffic on the roads. So, it will also help to reduce the work of traffic management department.
